Our

Vision

Our vision is to supply the students of UCL with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed as data scientists, while bringing them exciting opportunities to interact with top companies and leaders in the field in the form of events, talks and workshops

We believe that anyone with curiosity and basic quantitative skills can do data science. We seek to connect students with a passion of prediction, statistical learning and business intelligence.

4 steps to make you a great data scientist

Tailored Data Science curriculum

With UCL Data Science Curriculum we want to create a series of workshops which will equip students with the skills to be able to go right into data science jobs after graduating. Our model follow that of several established mini-programmes online, also called nano-degrees. This includes the following areas of know-how:

Proficiency in Python

Knowledge of analytics libraries

Making statistically sound decisions

Data Science consultancy

With the UCL Data Science Curriculum we will equip the students of UCL with vital skills for data science, however, there is a real difference between knowing and doing.

The Data Science Consulting provides students with the chance to work on real world, industry problems provided by top companies. This includes all kinds of exciting stuff:

Working on a data science project for a top company

+potential for internships&graduate schemes

Testing your data science skills by building predictive models for use in the real world

Careers

It is our aim to have our members pursuit a career in data science. By partnering up with leading companies, we want to give our members the opportunity to network and attend careers events.
